About Kriya

We manufacture and export high‑quality organic crop inputs—and partner with distributors to deliver reliable agronomic outcomes.

Who we are

Kriya Biosys Private Limited is a young and dynamic company specialized in the manufacture and export of premium organic crop inputs. Our purpose is to enable efficient nutrition management and robust pest & disease control while enhancing crop productivity—without harming nature or the environment.

Facilities
  • Manufacturing: International‑grade specs, stringent QC from raw material to dispatch.
  • R&D: Continuous product development and process updates to improve shelf‑life and bio‑efficacy.
  • Quality Control: Traceability from source to shipment; batch records monitored by a qualified QC team.

Mission

Advance sustainable agriculture by delivering certified, effective, and easy‑to‑use organic inputs that raise productivity and protect the environment.

Vision

To be the most trusted partner for distributors of organic pesticides, biostimulants, biofertilizers, and substrates—recognized for science‑led innovation, consistent quality, and dependable supply.

Values

  • Integrity: Transparent sourcing and consistent specifications.
  • Science: Decisions guided by data, analytics, and field validation.
  • Sustainability: Botanicals & beneficial microbes that reduce synthetic load.
  • Customer success: Distributor‑centric support and private‑label flexibility.
