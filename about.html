About Kriya Biosys

Premium, eco-friendly crop care built for professional distributors.

Who we are

Kriya Biosys is a B2B manufacturer focused on biocontrols, biostimulants, biofertilizers, and substrates. We combine discovery biology, advanced delivery systems, and responsible manufacturing to deliver reliable performance in the field.

Mission

To advance sustainable agriculture by unlocking botanical and microbial actives through superior formulation and delivery, making high-performing organic solutions accessible to distributors worldwide.

Vision

To be the most trusted partner for distributors seeking premium, residue-conscious products backed by science and consistent quality.

Values

  • Quality first — standardized actives, robust QC, consistency across batches.
  • Science-led — evidence-based R&D, rapid iteration, real-world validation.
  • Sustainability — eco-friendly inputs, responsible sourcing, safer user experience.
  • Partnership — distributor-centric support, private label, co-development.
