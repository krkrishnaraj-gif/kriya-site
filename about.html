About Kriya

We are a B2B manufacturer of premium, eco‑friendly crop care inputs for distributors worldwide. Our portfolio spans biocontrols, biostimulants, biofertilizers, substrates and home & garden products — all backed by rigorous R&D and quality manufacturing.

Mission

Advance sustainable agriculture by unlocking the potential of botanicals and microbes through superior delivery, stability and formulation science.

Vision

To be the most trusted partner for distributors — delivering reliable, residue‑conscious products that farmers love to use and retailers love to sell.

Values

  • Premium quality, uncompromised
  • Science first — validated performance
  • Compliance & stewardship
  • Reliable B2B partnerships

What we do

  • Microbial & botanical actives
  • Advanced formulation & delivery systems
  • Contract manufacturing (SC‑CO₂, extraction, fermentation, packing)
  • Analytical & application support
