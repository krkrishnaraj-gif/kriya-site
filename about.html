About Kriya

Kriya Biosys develops premium botanical and microbial crop inputs for professional distributors worldwide. We combine advanced delivery systems with responsible manufacturing to deliver reliable field performance.

Who We Are

Mission

Enable sustainable agriculture with effective, residue‑conscious biological inputs built on science‑led R&D and quality systems.

Vision

To be the most dependable B2B partner for biocontrols, biostimulants, biofertilizers and substrates across global markets.

Values

Quality, transparency, customer centricity, and stewardship — from sourcing to formulation to documentation.

What We Do

Botanical Actives

SC‑CO₂ and solvent/aqueous extraction with standardization for consistent potency.

Microbial Programs

Isolation, screening, fermentation, and metabolite workflows aligned to target pests and crop needs.

Formulation

Advanced delivery (micro/nano‑encapsulation), co‑formulants for adhesion, rainfastness and stability.

Documentation

B2B pack formats, product data sheets, and support for regulatory and quality audits.
