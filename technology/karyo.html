Karyo – Delivery Platform

Stability, handling, and efficacy for biological actives.

Overview

Karyo enhances botanical and microbial actives via micro/nano-encapsulation, UV protection, and controlled release, supported by synergistic co-formulants. The result is higher field performance with better user experience.

Core technologies

  • Micro & nano-encapsulation
  • Solubility enhancement & UV shielding
  • Adhesion & deposition improvement
  • Controlled release profiles

Use cases

  • Stabilizing delicate phytochemicals (e.g., limonoids)
  • Trans-laminar deposition and leaf surface retention
  • Compatibility with nutrition programs

Validation & results

Bench stability, field block trials, and batch-wise QC ensure consistent outcomes.
