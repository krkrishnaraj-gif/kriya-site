Technology

Karyo — Delivery Platform

Karyo unlocks the full potential of premium botanical and microbial actives through advanced delivery systems. We focus on stability, handling and field efficacy — using micro/nano‑encapsulation, solubility & dispersibility engineering, and synergistic co‑formulants designed for real‑world use.

What Karyo Solves

Stability

Protects sensitive actives against oxidation, light and hydrolysis; supports longer shelf‑life targets under varied storage conditions.

Handling

Improved flow, dispersibility and wetting; user‑friendly products that are easy to measure, mix and apply.

Efficacy

Optimized deposition, adhesion and retention on plant surfaces and in soil, driving consistent field performance.

Compatibility

Balanced co‑formulants and pH windows; built to play well in distributor portfolios and tank mixes (jar test recommended).

Core Technologies

Micro / Nano‑Encapsulation

Encapsulated botanicals and microbials for targeted protection and controlled release profiles.

Solubility & Dispersibility

Systems that improve wetting and suspendability for concentrates and powders in varied water qualities.

Synergistic Co‑Formulants

Carefully selected wetters, stickers and humectants that enhance deposition and rainfastness.

Stabilization Chemistries

Antioxidant systems, chelation, and buffering to safeguard active integrity during storage and use.

Pipeline

  1. 1

    Active Intake

    Botanical or microbial candidate intake with target profile, constraints, and regional goals.

  2. 2

    Fit‑for‑Purpose Design

    Define delivery route, co‑formulants, particle size, rheology, and stability targets.

  3. 3

    Prototype Build

    Bench‑scale micro/nano systems, dispersions and concentrates; early compatibility checks.

  4. 4

    Optimization

    Iterate on deposition, adhesion, pH windows and tank mix tolerance; stability studies begin.

  5. 5

    Scale‑Up

    Pilot → production with in‑process QC and reproducibility controls for consistent lots.

  6. 6

    Documentation

    Product specs, CoA templates and product data sheets supporting distributor workflows.
