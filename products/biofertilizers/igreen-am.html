IGreen AM

Bacillus Consortium — Rooting & Nutrient Efficiency

IGreen AM is a rhizobacteria blend of Bacillus amyloliquefaciens, B. velezensis and B. subtilis. The consortium colonizes the rhizosphere, promotes root growth and helps unlock nutrients, improving early vigor and resilience in vegetables, fruits and field crops.
