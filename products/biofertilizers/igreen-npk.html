IGreen NPK

Bacillus & Paenibacillus Consortium — Nutrient Efficiency

IGreen NPK brings together Bacillus megaterium, B. amyloliquefaciens, B. mucilaginosus and Paenibacillus azotofixans to enhance nutrient acquisition and plant performance. The consortium supports biological nitrogen fixation, phosphate solubilization and potassium mobilization, improving fertilizer efficiency and root vigor across diverse crops and soils.
