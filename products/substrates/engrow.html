Engrow

Seed Coating Agent

Engrow is a polymer‑based seed coating system designed to improve seed flowability, dust reduction and the uniform delivery of compatible actives (biocontrols, micronutrients and biostimulants). The coating forms a thin, uniform film that enhances planter metering and early stand establishment.

Use Directions

Charge: Add seed → mist polymer → add actives (if any).
Build film: Rotate until uniform sheen is achieved.
Dry: Air dry as per SOP; avoid overheating.
