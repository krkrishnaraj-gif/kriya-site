Maxineem

Neem Cake Substrate & Soil Amendment

Maxineem is a premium neem cake substrate produced from selected oil‑extracted kernels. It enhances soil organic matter, supports beneficial microbes, and improves nutrient use efficiency. Maxineem’s balanced carbon–nitrogen profile, inherent neem limonoids and uniform particle size make it a reliable backbone for nursery media, potting mixes and field incorporation programs.
