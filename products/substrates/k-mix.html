Karanja Cake Substrate

K‑Mix is a high‑quality karanja cake substrate designed for soil amendment and growing media enrichment. It contributes organic matter, improves water holding capacity and supports beneficial microbial activity. With clean sourcing and a consistent grind, K‑Mix blends easily into nursery and field programs.
