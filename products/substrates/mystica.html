Mystica

Silicone Wetting & Spreading Agent

Mystica is an advanced organo‑silicone super‑spreader that drastically reduces spray solution surface tension, enabling rapid wetting, superior leaf coverage and improved penetration of compatible foliar inputs. Use it to enhance performance of biocontrols, nutrients and biostimulants within validated tank mixes.
