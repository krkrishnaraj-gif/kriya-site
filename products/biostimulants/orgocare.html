Orgocare

Plant‑Extract Anti‑Transpirant & Anti‑Stress

Orgocare is a plant extract‑based anti‑transpirant designed to reduce excessive water loss and help plants tolerate heat and dry spells. It forms a micro‑film that moderates transpiration without blocking gas exchange, supporting canopy condition during stress windows.
