Zenita

Neem‑based Plant Growth Promoter (PGP)

Zenita is a potent neem‑based biostimulant formulated with water‑soluble limonoids and neem‑derived protein fractions. It supports early vigor, stronger rooting and improved nutrient utilization while helping plants manage environmental stress. Zenita blends cleanly and works alongside standard fertility programs in vegetables, fruits and ornamentals.
