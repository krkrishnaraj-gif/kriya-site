Enrhize

AM Fungal Consortium — 1000 spores/g

Enrhize contains a consortium of arbuscular mycorrhizal (AM) fungi — Glomus aggregatum, G. mosseae, and G. intraradices — at 1000 spores per gram. It promotes extensive root systems, improves phosphorus and micronutrient capture, and enhances water relations for stronger, more resilient crops.
