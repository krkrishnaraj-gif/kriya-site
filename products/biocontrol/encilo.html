EnCilo

Paecilomyces fumosoroseus — EC & WP

EnCilo contains Paecilomyces fumosoroseus, an entomopathogenic fungus used to manage soft‑bodied pests such as whiteflies, aphids and mealybugs. Spores adhere to the insect cuticle, germinate and colonize internally, leading to mortality. EnCilo is formulated in high‑viability EC and WP formats for stable handling, dispersion across practical water qualities, and dependable deposition on foliage—supporting consistent results in IPM rotations when used as directed under local labels.

Use Directions

Mix: EC — add slowly to partially filled tank under agitation; WP — pre‑slurry if needed and add to tank.
Water: Clean water; moderate pH range (≈6–7.5) preferred.
Timing: Apply during cooler periods; avoid heat/drought stress windows.
Coverage: Ensure leaf undersides and pest habitats are wetted.

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test small mixtures before tank‑mixing at scale.
Avoid: Strong oxidizers; highly alkaline mixes unless validated.
