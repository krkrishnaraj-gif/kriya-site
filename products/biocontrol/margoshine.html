Margoshine

Neem Oil 70% (EC)

Margoshine is a premium horticultural oil built on highly refined neem oil (70%) and a balanced EC system for easy dilution, uniform coverage, and dependable field performance. The formulation is engineered for smooth handling in distributor programs, robust emulsification in a range of water qualities, and excellent wetting to reach pest hot‑spots. It fits well in IPM rotations and supports a clean, crop‑friendly finish when used as directed under local guidelines.

Use Directions

Mix: Add slowly to a partially filled tank under agitation; top up with water.
Water: Clean water; pH 6–7.5 preferred for emulsion stability.
Timing: Apply in cooler parts of the day; avoid heat/drought stress windows.
Coverage: Ensure thorough contact with target surfaces.
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test before tank‑mixing.
Avoid: Strong oxidizers; highly alkaline mixes unless validated.
Caution: Separate timing from sulfur & some foliar fertilizers.
Follow: Local crop safety restrictions and PHIs.
