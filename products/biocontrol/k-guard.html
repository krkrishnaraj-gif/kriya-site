K‑Guard

Karanjin Botanical Insecticide (EC & WSP)

K‑Guard is a botanical biopesticide based on karanjin, a furanoflavonoid concentrated from *Pongamia pinnata* (karanja). Karanjin is widely documented for insecticidal and acaricidal activity and has strong antifeedant/repellent effects, making it a practical tool in integrated programs when used as directed. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} Our EC and WSP variants are engineered for stable dilution/dispersion, robust coverage and portfolio fit in distributor workflows. Botanical origin and biodegradability support eco‑friendly positioning where locally permitted. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Use Directions

Mixing (EC): Add slowly to a partially filled tank under agitation; top up with water.
Mixing (WSP): Add sachet to water; pre‑slurry if needed for cold water; maintain gentle agitation.
Water quality: Clean water; moderate pH preferred (≈6–7.5).
Timing & coverage: Apply in cooler parts of the day; ensure thorough, even wetting.
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test before tank‑mixing with other inputs.
Avoid: Strong oxidizers and highly alkaline mixes unless validated.
Rotation: Integrate with other tools as part of IPM and resistance management. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
