K‑Guard

Karanjin Botanical Biopesticide

K‑Guard contains karanjin, a furanoflavonoid naturally occurring in Pongamia pinnata (karanja). It is known for antifeedant, repellency and insect growth‑regulating activity on key pests, while aligning well with IPM rotations. The K‑Guard line is engineered for stable handling, water compatibility and deposition on foliage, supporting consistent program performance when used as directed under local regulations.

Use Directions

Mix: EC — add slowly into a partially filled tank under agitation; WSP — pre‑slurry if needed and add to tank.
Water: Use clean water; moderate pH (approx. 6–7.5) typically preferred.
Timing: Apply in cooler parts of the day; avoid heat/drought stress windows.
Coverage: Ensure thorough contact with target surfaces (including undersides).
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test small mixtures before tank‑mixing at scale.
Avoid: Strong oxidizers; highly alkaline mixes unless validated.
Rotate: Integrate with complementary tools for resistance management.
Observe: Local label specifics, crop lists and PHIs.
