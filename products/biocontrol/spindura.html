Use Directions

Mix: Add to a partially filled tank under agitation; top up with water.
Water: Clean water; moderate pH range (6–7.5) generally preferred.
Timing: Apply in cooler parts of day; avoid heat/drought stress windows.
Coverage: Ensure thorough contact with target surfaces.
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test before tank‑mixing; follow local label instructions.
Avoid: Strong oxidizers; highly alkaline mixes unless validated.
Rotation: Integrate with other tools to support resistance management.
Observe: Local crop safety restrictions and PHIs.
