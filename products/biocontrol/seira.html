Seira

Verticillium lecanii — EC & WP

Seira contains Verticillium lecanii, an entomopathogenic fungus effective against whiteflies, aphids and thrips. It parasitizes soft-bodied insects by adhering to the cuticle, germinating and colonizing internally. With high spore count EC and WP formats, Seira delivers consistent performance and integrates well into IPM programs.
