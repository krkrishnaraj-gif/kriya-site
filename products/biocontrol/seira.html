Biocontrol

Seira

Verticillium lecanii — EC & WP

Seira contains Verticillium lecanii, an entomopathogenic fungus effective against whiteflies, aphids and thrips. Spores adhere to the insect cuticle, germinate, and colonize internally—leading to pest mortality. With high spore count EC and WP formats, Seira provides consistent field results and fits smoothly into IPM programs when used as directed under local labels.
