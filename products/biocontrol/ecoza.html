Use Directions

Mix: Add slowly to a partially filled tank under agitation; top up with water.
Water: Use clean water; moderate pH 6–7.5 preferred.
Timing: Apply during cooler day parts; avoid stress windows.
Coverage: Ensure uniform spray and thorough target contact.
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always validate tank mixes; avoid strong oxidizers.
Spacing: Separate from sulfur and some foliar fertilizers.
Local labels: Follow regional crop safety restrictions and PHIs.
