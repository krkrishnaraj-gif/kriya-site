WeedX

Non-selective Contact Herbicide

WeedX is a fast-acting, contact herbicide for burndown of emerged grasses and broadleaf weeds. It disrupts the integrity of the weed cuticle on contact, causing rapid desiccation of green tissues. WeedX is formulated for consistent handling, practical mixing in field water, and reliable foliar deposition when used as directed under local regulations and labels. Best performance is achieved on young, actively growing weeds with thorough spray coverage.

Use Directions

Timing: Treat emerged weeds; younger weeds are easier to control.
Coverage: Ensure uniform wetting; adjust nozzles for medium-coarse droplets.
Water: Use clean water; avoid very hard/alkaline water unless validated.
Repeat: Re-treat regrowth as needed according to local label intervals.
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test small mixtures, especially with oils/adjuvants.
Avoid: Spraying when rain is imminent; allow sufficient rainfast period.
Shield: Prevent contact with desirable green tissues and bark cracks.
Observe: Local label rates, PPE and buffer zone requirements.
