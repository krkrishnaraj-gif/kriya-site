Mycova

Beauveria bassiana — EC & WP

Mycova is a biological insecticide containing Beauveria bassiana, an entomopathogenic fungus used to manage soft‑bodied and certain foliar and soil insects. It is formulated in both EC and WP formats with high conidia viability to support consistent performance, wetting and coverage. The formulation system focuses on stability, water compatibility and deposition on foliage to help distributors run dependable programs when used as directed under local guidance.

Use Directions

Mix: EC — add slowly to a partially filled tank under agitation; WP — pre‑slurry if needed and add to tank.
Water: Use clean water; moderate pH range (≈6–7.5) generally preferred.
Timing: Apply in cooler parts of the day; avoid heat/drought stress windows.
Coverage: Ensure thorough contact with target surfaces, including leaf undersides.
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test small mixtures before tank‑mixing at scale.
Avoid: Strong oxidizers; highly alkaline mixes unless validated.
Rotate: Integrate with complementary tools for resistance management.
Observe: Local label specifics, crop lists and PHIs.
