Biocontrol

Biota

Trichoderma viride (Biota-V) & Trichoderma harzianum (Biota-H)

Biota is a line of Trichoderma-based biological fungicides for managing soil-borne and foliar fungal diseases. Available in EC and WP formulations, Biota-V (Trichoderma viride) and Biota-H (Trichoderma harzianum) each deliver high spore viability, soil colonization ability, and competitive exclusion of pathogens. These products are engineered for consistent performance, tank mix tolerance, and compatibility in IPM programs.

Biota-V (T. viride)

Available in EC and WP formats — 1×108 viable conidia per mL (EC) or g (WP).
