Neuvita

Ampelomyces quisqualis — EC & WP

Neuvita contains Ampelomyces quisqualis, a mycoparasitic fungus that attacks and suppresses powdery mildew. It colonizes powdery mildew structures, penetrating hyphae and pycnidia to reduce disease pressure. With high spore count EC and WP formulations, Neuvita is engineered for stability, dispersion and practical deposition—fitting seamlessly into integrated disease management programs under local labels.

Use Directions

Mix: EC — add slowly to a partially filled tank under agitation; WP — pre‑slurry if needed and add to tank.
Water: Clean water; moderate pH range (≈6–7.5) preferred.
Timing: Apply preventively or at first sign of disease; repeat as directed.
Coverage: Thoroughly wet infected surfaces; ensure canopy penetration.
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test small mixtures before tank‑mixing at scale.
Avoid: Strong fungicidal chemistries at time of application unless validated.
Rotate: Combine with complementary modes of action for durability.
Observe: Local label specifics and PHIs.
