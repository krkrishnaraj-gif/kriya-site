Rexora

Metarhizium anisopliae — EC & WP

Rexora is a biological insecticide containing Metarhizium anisopliae, an entomopathogenic fungus used to manage a range of soil‑dwelling and foliar insect pests. Offered in EC and WP formats with high conidia viability, Rexora is formulated for stable handling, water compatibility and adhesion on foliage and root zones. It integrates smoothly into IPM rotations and supports consistent field performance when used as directed under local labels and guidance.

Use Directions

Mix: EC — add slowly to a partially filled tank under agitation; WP — pre‑slurry if needed and add to tank.
Water: Use clean water; moderate pH range (≈6–7.5) generally preferred.
Timing: Apply in cooler parts of the day; avoid heat/drought stress windows.
Coverage: Ensure thorough contact with target surfaces (leaf undersides/soil zone).
⚗

Directions & Compatibility

Jar test: Always test small mixtures before tank‑mixing at scale.
Avoid: Strong oxidizers; highly alkaline mixes unless validated.
Rotate: Integrate with complementary tools for resistance management.
Observe: Local label specifics, crop lists and PHIs.
